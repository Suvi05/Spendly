Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service to you — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling filtering and search. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect it from unauthorized access. Your expense data is associated only with your account and is not accessible to other users. You may request full deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party services. We may use third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the platform, but these providers do not have access to your financial data beyond what is necessary to run the service.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request deletion of your data, please reach out through the contact information available on our site. We will respond to all privacy-related requests promptly.
